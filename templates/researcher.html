Holy Roman Empire

Asimov was inspired by the downfall of the holy roman empire. The Holy Roman Empire and Galactic Empire in foundation were huge and stable empires on the surface, but the corruption and structural weaknesses led to their downfall. After the downfall of the holy roman empire, science and knowledge were only preserved by the church, commoners at the time viewed natural phenomena as mericle, which is similar to how foundation present itself as a religion.
