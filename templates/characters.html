Character Profile

Hari Seldon

The Psychohistorian

Hari Seldon is the creator of psychohistory and the foundation. With psychohistory, he discovers that the Galactic Empire will fall in 300 years, and creates the foundation with the goal of shortening the dark age from 30000 years to only 1000 years. Although he passes away after the first part of the book, he creates a vault where he provides information to future members of the foundation every few years to guide them in their journey. However, he doesn’t inform the foundation of the true purpose they’re supposed to fulfil or else, according to Seldon, the foundation’s plan will crumble.

Character Profile

Salvor Hardin

The Pragmatist

Salvor Hardon is the mayor of Terminus, the planet that the foundation resides in, 50 years after Seldon’s passing. Instead of focusing directly on research like the rest of the foundation, he believes that to keep Terminus alive, they need to take action politically. However, he does not defeat threats and surrounding kingdoms with violence, but rather with manipulation through religious control.

Character Profile

Hober Mallow

The Merchant

Hober Mallow becomes one of the foundation’s most successful leaders as he uses his knowledge in trade. Spreading Foundation’s goods and knowledge to other kingdoms, he realizes that others begin to depend on resources and products from the Foundation, allowing them the Foundation to grow.

Character Profile

Bel Riose

The Soldier

Bel Riose is a military commander in the deteriorating Galactic Empire towards the end of the book. He shows to be a great, competent leader capable of defeating the Foundation. However, the Empire itself prevents them from being able to defeat the Foundation as the Emperor fears that Riose may become too powerful and overthrow him, leading to his arrest. Without Riose, the Empire was unable to defeat the Foundation.
